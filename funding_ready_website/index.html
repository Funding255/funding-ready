Welcome to Funding Ready

Empowering Tanzanian Women to Build Fearless Businesses. At Funding Ready, we believe every woman deserves the confidence and tools to launch and grow her business without fear. We guide future and small business owners through essential steps—helping you learn how to register your business, access funding, save wisely, and build strong foundations for long-term success.

Our Mission

To empower Tanzanian women with the knowledge, resources, and connections they need to confidently start and sustain their businesses.

Our Values

  • Empowerment: Every woman deserves the chance to thrive in business.
  • Accessibility: Affordable guidance tailored to real Tanzanian needs.
  • Sustainability: Building businesses that last for generations.
  • Community: Connecting women to opportunities and to each other.
